Description

Spanish omelette, also known as tortilla espa√±ola or tortilla de patatas, is a traditional dish from Spain that is typically made with potatoes, onions, and eggs.

The basic recipe for a Spanish omelette includes potatoes, onion, eggs, olive oil, salt and pepper.

Ingredients

  • 6 medium potatoes
  • 1/2 onion, diced
  • Salt and pepper
  • 6 eggs
  • /4 cup olive oil
  • 1/4 cup diced chorizo (optional)
  • 1/4 cup diced bell pepper (optional)
  • Peel and slice the potatoes into thin rounds.
  • In a large skillet, heat olive oil over medium heat.
  • Add the potato slices, diced onion, chorizo (if using), and bell pepper (if using) and cook until potatoes are tender, about 10 minutes.
  • Season with salt and pepper to taste.
  • Beat the eggs in a bowl and pour over the potato mixture.
  • Cook until the bottom is set, about 5 minutes.
  • Use a spatula to flip the omelette, and cook the other side until set, about 3 more minutes.
