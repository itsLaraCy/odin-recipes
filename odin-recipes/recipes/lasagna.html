Description

Lasagna is a traditional Italian dish that is typically made with layers of pasta, meat, and cheese.

The basic recipe for lasagna includes lasagna noodles, ground beef, onion, garlic, marinara sauce, ricotta cheese, egg, parmesan cheese, and mozzarella cheese.

Ingredients

  • 12 lasagna noodles
  • 1 ground beef
  • 1 onion, diced
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 (24 oz) jar marinara sauce
  • 1 (15 oz) container ricotta cheese
  • 1 egg
  • 1/4 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 2 cups shredded mozzarella cheese
  • Salt and pepper, to taste
